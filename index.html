About me

Well, hello there! I am humbled and honored to find out that you want to know more about me. Let's get this going then!

My name is Shoaib Shaikh, but my friends call me Sho. Born in India, raised in New York, and currently residing in California, I am in search the next big thing in my life. Always being passionate about technology, I dove head first into the crazy world of programming and instantly fell in love with it. I love the long hours of coding, trying to solve highly complicated problems, almost giving up and then all of sudden there's a breakthrough. That feeling you get after solving a problem is unmatched.

Other than being a web developer, I am also a gamer. Always have been, always will be. Some of the games I play currently are Apex Legends, Valorant, Hearthstone, and Minecraft. If you play any of these, hit me up!
